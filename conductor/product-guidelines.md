# Product Guidelines

## Narrative & Tone
- **Dynamic Selection:** Upon starting a new game, the AI should generate a variety of narrative tone suggestions (e.g., dark fantasy, space comedy, noir mystery) for the player to choose from, allowing for a personalized adventure.
- **Cohesive Storytelling:** The AI must consistently adhere to the selected tone throughout the game session, ensuring a unified narrative voice and atmosphere.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Dynamic & Immersive:** The user interface should feel alive and responsive, using animations, transitions, and interactive feedback to deepen the player's immersion.
- **Narrative-Centric Design:** The UI should be designed to highlight the AI-generated story and images, ensuring that the text remains legible and the visuals are impactful.

## AI Generation Principles
- **Player-Influenced Narratives:** The game should strike a balance between AI-driven suggestions and player freedom. While the AI provides paths, players should be able to input custom text actions to influence the story's direction.
- **Seamless Multi-modal Integration:** AI-generated text and images must be tightly coupled, with visuals accurately reflecting the specific narrative context and atmosphere described in the text.

## Visual Language
- **Stylized Illustrations:** All AI-generated imagery should follow a consistent, vibrant, and stylized illustrative art style (e.g., digital painting, expressive oil painting) rather than photorealism.
- **Atmospheric Visuals:** Visuals should prioritize mood and atmosphere over literal representation, serving to enhance the emotional weight of the story.
